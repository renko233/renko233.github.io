归一化不随时间改变

归一化的表达式为

∫−∞∞∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=1\int _{-\infty}^{\infty}|\Psi (x,t)|^2 dx =1 ∫−∞∞​∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=1

由于该式为对 xxx 的积分, 则归一化随时间的变化可写为

ddt∫−∞∞∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=∫−∞∞∂∂t∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx\frac{d}{dt}\int _{-\infty}^{\infty}|\Psi (x,t)|^2 dx=\int _{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\Psi (x,t)|^2 dx dtd​∫−∞∞​∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=∫−∞∞​∂t∂​∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx

其中

∂∂t∣Ψ∣2=∂∂t(Ψ∗Ψ)=Ψ∗∂Ψ∂t+∂Ψ∗∂tΨ\frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\Psi |^2=\frac{\partial}{\partial t}(\Psi ^{*}\Psi)=\Psi ^{*}\frac{\partial \Psi}{\partial t}+ \frac{\partial \Psi^{*}}{\partial t} \Psi ∂t∂​∣Ψ∣2=∂t∂​(Ψ∗Ψ)=Ψ∗∂t∂Ψ​+∂t∂Ψ∗​Ψ

由薛定谔方程及其共轭式有

∂Ψ∂t=iℏ2m∂2Ψ∂x2−iℏVΨ\frac{\partial \Psi}{\partial t}=\frac{i\hbar}{2m}\frac{\partial ^2 \Psi}{\partial x^2}-\frac{i}{\hbar}V\Psi ∂t∂Ψ​=2miℏ​∂x2∂2Ψ​−ℏi​VΨ

∂Ψ∗∂t=−iℏ2m∂2Ψ∗∂x2+iℏVΨ∗\frac{\partial \Psi ^{*}}{\partial t}=-\frac{i\hbar}{2m}\frac{\partial ^2 \Psi ^{*}}{\partial x^2}+\frac{i}{\hbar}V\Psi ^{*} ∂t∂Ψ∗​=−2miℏ​∂x2∂2Ψ∗​+ℏi​VΨ∗

于是

∂∂t∣Ψ∣2=iℏ2m(Ψ∗∂2Ψ∂x2−∂2Ψ∗∂x2Ψ)=∂∂x[iℏ2m(Ψ∗∂Ψ∂x−∂Ψ∗∂xΨ)]\frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\Psi |^2=\frac{i\hbar}{2m}(\Psi ^{*}\frac{\partial ^2 \Psi}{\partial x^2}-\frac{\partial ^2 \Psi ^{*}}{\partial x^2}\Psi) =\frac{\partial}{\partial x}[\frac{i\hbar}{2m}(\Psi ^* \frac{\partial \Psi}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial \Psi ^*}{\partial x}\Psi)] ∂t∂​∣Ψ∣2=2miℏ​(Ψ∗∂x2∂2Ψ​−∂x2∂2Ψ∗​Ψ)=∂x∂​[2miℏ​(Ψ∗∂x∂Ψ​−∂x∂Ψ∗​Ψ)]

则随时间变化的积分可写作

ddt∫−∞∞∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=iℏ2m(Ψ∗∂Ψ∂x−∂Ψ∗∂xΨ)∣−∞∞\frac{d}{dt} \int _{-\infty}^{\infty}|\Psi (x,t)|^2 dx=\frac{i\hbar}{2m}(\Psi ^* \frac{\partial \Psi}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial \Psi ^*}{\partial x}\Psi)|_{-\infty}^{\infty} dtd​∫−∞∞​∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=2miℏ​(Ψ∗∂x∂Ψ​−∂x∂Ψ∗​Ψ)∣−∞∞​

又由于波函数要可归一化, 即 xxx 趋于无穷时 Ψ\PsiΨ 要趋于零, 即:

ddt∫−∞∞∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=0\frac{d}{dt} \int _{-\infty}^{\infty}|\Psi (x,t)|^2 dx=0 dtd​∫−∞∞​∣Ψ(x,t)∣2dx=0

即归一化不随时间改变.

概率流密度

在 “归一化不随时间改变” 中已得到过下式

∂∂t∣Ψ∣2=∂∂x[iℏ2m(Ψ∗∂Ψ∂x−∂Ψ∗∂xΨ)]\frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\Psi |^2=\frac{\partial}{\partial x}[\frac{i\hbar}{2m}(\Psi ^* \frac{\partial \Psi}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial \Psi ^*}{\partial x}\Psi)] ∂t∂​∣Ψ∣2=∂x∂​[2miℏ​(Ψ∗∂x∂Ψ​−∂x∂Ψ∗​Ψ)]

若换成三维情况, 且令概率 P=∣Ψ∣2P=|\Psi |^2P=∣Ψ∣2 ,则有

∂P∂t=iℏ2m∇⋅(Ψ∗∇Ψ−Ψ∇Ψ∗)\frac{\partial P}{\partial t}=\frac{i\hbar}{2m}\nabla\cdot (\Psi ^* \nabla \Psi - \Psi \nabla \Psi ^* ) ∂t∂P​=2miℏ​∇⋅(Ψ∗∇Ψ−Ψ∇Ψ∗)

若令

J=iℏ2m∇⋅(Ψ∇Ψ∗−Ψ∗∇Ψ)J=\frac{i\hbar}{2m}\nabla\cdot ( \Psi \nabla \Psi ^* - \Psi ^* \nabla \Psi ) J=2miℏ​∇⋅(Ψ∇Ψ∗−Ψ∗∇Ψ)

则有

∂P∂t+∇⋅J=0\frac{\partial P}{\partial t}+\nabla \cdot J =0 ∂t∂P​+∇⋅J=0

显然这是一个连续性方程的微分形式, 若将其作体积分则有

∭∂P∂tdV=−∭∇⋅JdV\iiint \frac{\partial P}{\partial t} dV=-\iiint \nabla \cdot J dV ∭∂t∂P​dV=−∭∇⋅JdV

由高斯公式(见本博客的矢量分析那一篇)可将散度的体积分换成矢量的曲面积分,即

∭∂P∂tdV=−\oiintJdS\iiint \frac{\partial P}{\partial t} dV=-\oiint J dS ∭∂t∂P​dV=−∬​JdS

即单位时间内曲面所包围的体积内的概率增加等于单位时间概率流密度通过曲面进入的面积分, 也就是概率. 其中 JJJ 就定义为概率流密度

⟨α∣Q^α⟩=a⟨α∣α⟩\langle \alpha | \hat{Q} \alpha \rangle =a \langle \alpha |\alpha \rangle ⟨α∣Q^​α⟩=a⟨α∣α⟩

⟨α∣Q^α⟩=⟨Q^α∣α⟩\langle \alpha | \hat{Q} \alpha \rangle = \langle \hat{Q} \alpha | \alpha \rangle ⟨α∣Q^​α⟩=⟨Q^​α∣α⟩

a⟨α∣α⟩=a∗⟨α∣α⟩a \langle \alpha |\alpha \rangle = a^{*} \langle \alpha |\alpha \rangle a⟨α∣α⟩=a∗⟨α∣α⟩

a=a∗a=a^{*} a=a∗

即厄米算符的本征值为实数, 证毕.

Ehrenfest 定理

Ehrenfest 定理为

ddt⟨Q^⟩=1iℏ⟨[Q^,H^]⟩+⟨∂Q^∂t⟩\frac{d}{dt} \langle \hat{Q} \rangle =\frac{1}{i \hbar} \langle [\hat{Q},\hat{H}] \rangle +\langle \frac{\partial \hat{Q}}{\partial t}\rangle dtd​⟨Q^​⟩=iℏ1​⟨[Q^​,H^]⟩+⟨∂t∂Q^​​⟩

Virial 定理

Virial 定理为:

2⟨T⟩=⟨r⋅∇V(r)⟩2 \langle T \rangle =\langle r \cdot \nabla V(r) \rangle 2⟨T⟩=⟨r⋅∇V(r)⟩

证明方式(1):

若 EEE 不显含 λ\lambdaλ , 而 H^(λx)\hat{H}(\lambda x)H^(λx) , ψ(λx)\psi (\lambda x)ψ(λx) 与 λ\lambdaλ 相关,则

∂E∂λ=0\frac{\partial E}{\partial \lambda} =0 ∂λ∂E​=0

由Hellmann–Feynman 定理得

0=⟨ψ∣∂H^(λx)∂λ∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣∂∂λ(−ℏ22md2d(λx)2+V(λx))∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣∂∂λ(−1λ2ℏ22md2dx2+V(λx))∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣2λ3ℏ22md2dx2+∂V∂(λx)∂λx∂λ∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣2λ3ℏ22md2dx2+x∂V∂(λx)∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣2ℏ22md2dx2+x∂V∂x∣ψ⟩令λ=1=⟨ψ∣−2T^+x∂V∂x∣ψ⟩=−2⟨T⟩+⟨x∂V∂x⟩\begin{aligned} 0 &= \langle \psi |\frac{\partial \hat{H}(\lambda x)}{\partial \lambda} | \psi \rangle \\ &= \langle \psi | \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}(-\frac{\hbar ^2}{2m}\frac{d^2}{d(\lambda x)^2}+V(\lambda x))|\psi \rangle \\ &= \langle \psi | \frac{\partial}{\partial \lambda}(-\frac{1}{\lambda ^2}\frac{\hbar ^2}{2m}\frac{d^2}{dx^2}+V(\lambda x))|\psi \rangle \\ &=\langle \psi |\frac{2}{\lambda ^3}\frac{\hbar ^2}{2m}\frac{d^2}{dx^2}+\frac{\partial V}{\partial (\lambda x)}\frac{\partial {\lambda x}}{\partial \lambda} |\psi \rangle \\ &= \langle \psi |\frac{2}{\lambda ^3}\frac{\hbar ^2}{2m}\frac{d^2}{dx^2}+x \frac{\partial V}{\partial (\lambda x)} |\psi \rangle \\ &= \langle \psi |2\frac{\hbar ^2}{2m}\frac{d^2}{dx^2}+x \frac{\partial V}{\partial x} |\psi \rangle \qquad 令 \lambda =1 \\ &= \langle \psi |-2 \hat{T} +x\frac{\partial V}{\partial x} |\psi \rangle \\ &=-2 \langle T \rangle + \langle x \frac{\partial V}{\partial x} \rangle \end{aligned} 0​=⟨ψ∣∂λ∂H^(λx)​∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣∂λ∂​(−2mℏ2​d(λx)2d2​+V(λx))∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣∂λ∂​(−λ21​2mℏ2​dx2d2​+V(λx))∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣λ32​2mℏ2​dx2d2​+∂(λx)∂V​∂λ∂λx​∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣λ32​2mℏ2​dx2d2​+x∂(λx)∂V​∣ψ⟩=⟨ψ∣22mℏ2​dx2d2​+x∂x∂V​∣ψ⟩令λ=1=⟨ψ∣−2T^+x∂x∂V​∣ψ⟩=−2⟨T⟩+⟨x∂x∂V​⟩​
